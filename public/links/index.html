Links 🔗

Useful links, cool blogs, etc…

📖 Houdini Documentation

Houdini manual.

📹 REF FX

Great site with lots of real world and CG FX references.

👥 od|force Forums

Go-to place for community input.

🐸 Toadstorm Nerdblog

Henry Foster's amazing blog. Some of the best guides for the stuff you really need to know about.

👾 Juraj Tomori

Really cool TD blog. HDA versioning and Rain Instancing write-ups are great. Also has an awesome GitHub.

👾 Eetu daily.hip

Many great hip files.

👾 CG Wiki

The OG Houdini instructor. Best Gifs around.

☁️ Sergen Eren

Really great writeups on clouds.

🧶 Entagma

Another amazing all-around Houdini learning site.

👾 Houdini Gubbins

Super technical, way above my head. Really neat papers and implementations.

👾 Fredrik Averpil

Very helpful python stuff here. Inspiration for my site's current PaperMod Hugo theme too.

🖥️ Houdini Blueprints

Run Houdini setups interactively in a browser.

📈 Easings

Cheat sheet for keyframe tangents.

📸 HDRI Haven

A place with many high-quality HDRIs for lighting.

🐛 Rich Lord

Lots of fun setups. Some cool locomotion stuff with RBDs and Vellum.

🇯🇵 Junchiro Horikawa

Cool channel, very vex-focused. His Algorithmic design lessons are top-notch.

🔥 Peter Claes

Great instructor, amazing learning content.

🤯 XAPKOHHEH

Mindblowing setups. Check out what really goes on when you animate a cube.

👾 Ryoji CG Memo

So many useful snippets. Found myself visiting this frequently starting out.

🔮 Shadertoy

Many implementations of ridiculously cool shaders. Not sure where all these people are, but there are tons of cool shaders to look at here.

🍃 Nature of Code

Free e-book written to explore coding with Processing. Very interesting.

📕 Red Blob Games

Great breakdowns of algorithms and whitepapers.

📗 The Book of Shaders

Another free e-book about writing shaders. Have only just started it.

🔦 Chris Brejon - CG Cinamatography Archives

Another book-style site that covers more of the artistic side of CG lighting.

🎥 Zeno Pelgrims

Original creator of shaders.xyz! Now writing cool imagers for Arnold, including Lentil.

➗ Fundza

Interesting CG math coding tutorials and examples.

👾 Andreas Böinghoff

Another cool blog about VOPs.

∿ Tuxedo Labs - Blue Noise

Writeup about noise.

💪 Lee Griggs - Arnold Rendering Blog

Cool blog from one of the Arnold devs.

💪 Steven Blair - Arnold Support Blog

Another blog from Arnold dev Steven Blair. Really useful writeups, especially the one about visibility bitmasking.

☢️ Nukepedia

Go-to place for Nuke plugins and gizmos. Make sure you get an account so can you download stuff.

☢️ Comp Fu

Various Nuke blog posts and tutorials.

👾 Doctor Hou Doodles

Another blog with some interesting setups.

👾 CG Render DNA

Very similar layout to Doctor Hou Doodles above, but more rendering-oriented.

👗 I, Houdini

Really cool write up about cloth tearing in Houdini.

🇯🇵 Quiita (Japanese)

Japanese blog with some useful info about Alembic.

🇯🇵 Dear Houdini (Japanese)

Another Japanese blog with a lot of vex snippets.

🎨 Heribert Raab

Some cool rendering art.

🎨 Heribert Raab (Blog)

Blog related to the above link.

🔥 Sadjad Rabiee

Awesome youtube channel with tons of great Pyro techniques.
